Rules for alphabets

1.Front Side of the Card: The front side of the card will display a letter of the alphabet in a large font.

2.Back Side of the Card: When you click on the letter card, it will flip to the back side. The back side will display: An image corresponding to the letter (e.g., an apple image for the letter "Aa"). The word associated with the letter (e.g., "Apple" for the letter "Aa"). If click the image to hear the pronunciation of the word in British English accent

3.Shuffled Alphabet Order: The alphabet cards will appear in a shuffled order, not in the correct alphabetical sequence.

4.Starting Card: The app will start with a randomly selected alphabet card.

5.Letter Representation: Uppercase and lowercase letters will be used to represent the alphabet.

Alphabet Drag and Drop Quiz Game

1.The game consists of a set of alphabet images and an empty word container.

2.The alphabet images are draggable and can be moved by clicking and dragging them

3.The player's task is to match the correct word to the displayed alphabet image by dragging and dropping the image over the empty word container.

4.Each time an alphabet image is dropped, the game checks if the word matches the correct word for that alphabet.

5.If the word matches correctly, the word container will be filled with the word, and the next alphabet image will appear.

6.If the word does not match the correct word for the alphabet, the image will return to its original position, and the player can try again.

7.The game continues until all alphabet images have been matched with their correct words.
